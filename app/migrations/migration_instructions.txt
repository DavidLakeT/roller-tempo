1. Migrations will help to manage the db of the app



2. add_migration, this will help us create new migration files 

    run: sh add_migration.sh "Name of new migration"

    

3. up_migration, this will help us to execute our migrations on the db

    run: sh up_migrations.sh



4. down_migration, this will help us to remove all our migrations from the db

    run: sh down_migrations.sh 

    

5. force_migration, when encountering an unexpected error when executing up migration execute this command

    run: sh force_migrations.sh
